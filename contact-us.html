About Us

My name is Ethan Champion and I created this newsletter in December 2018. The newsletter came about after realising that there was very little centralised newsletters for the topic of Artificial Intelligence.

The goal of this website was to create a demo about a potential newsletter, including creating templates for news articles, and simplified methods of creating new articles.

This website uses a Boilerplate template, a Bootstrap framework for layout, and jQuery for inserting news articles with ease. It is up to date with HTML5 and CSS3 standards.

The website is responsive for all devices and has been tested for most popular devices.
